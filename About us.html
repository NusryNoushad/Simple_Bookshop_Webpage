About Us

History

ABC Bookstore has been serving the community of Kandy for over 20 years. It was founded by Mr. John Smith in 2002 with a vision to provide a wide range of books and promote the love for reading.

Vision

Our vision is to be the go-to destination for book lovers in Kandy and beyond. We strive to create a welcoming and enriching environment where people can explore and discover their favorite books.

Mission

Our mission is to offer a diverse selection of high-quality books, including bestsellers, classics, academic textbooks, and more. We aim to provide exceptional customer service and foster a passion for reading among people of all ages.
